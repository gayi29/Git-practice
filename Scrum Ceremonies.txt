Scrum methodology:

Scrum Ceremony - Breaking larger projects into small manageable pieces of work.

Roles:

1. Product Owner
2. Scrum master
3. team members

Scrum Ceremonies - Mainly four

a. Sprint Planning

	- to plan the project and all should attend this meeting.

b. Daily Scrum

	- to connect everyone & discuss up to date on the progress.
	
	eg: What did I work on yesterday?
	    What did I work on today?

c. Sprint review

	- to review everything that is accomplished during last sprint

d. Sprint retrospective

	- Discuss on improvements.
